About Seoul Delights

Feel the Seoul taste bud!

Our brand is built on the idea of blending traditional Korean recipes with a contemporary twist, creating a unique dining experience that appeals to both connoisseurs of Korean cuisine and those new to its rich flavors. The Seoul Delight is a culinary journey that brings the vibrant and diverse flavors of Korean cuisine to a modern and dynamic setting.

About Chef

Jiwon Ku

Chef Ji-Won Ku, a Seoul native, developed a passion for cooking from an early age, inspired by the vibrant street food and rich culinary traditions of South Korea. After completing culinary school in Seoul, Chef Ku embarked on a culinary journey that took her to various kitchens across the globe, from the bustling markets of Seoul to the Michelin-starred restaurants in Paris.

She is known for her dedication to preserving the authenticity of Korean flavors while embracing a modern and innovative approach to cooking. She believes in the importance of using high-quality, seasonal ingredients to create dishes that not only taste exquisite but also reflect the beauty of each season.
